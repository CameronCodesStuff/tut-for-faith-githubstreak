#🔥 GitHub Streak Tutorial 6

> A quick guide to keep my GitHub contribution streak alive while i am on the plane ✈️

---

## 📋 Steps

1. **Open the file**
   Click on `index.html` — you'll also see instructions at the top of the file.

2. **Enter edit mode**
   Tap the ✏️ pencil icon to edit.
   > 💡 *Tip: you may need to rotate your phone sideways to see the icon.*

3. **Find the line**
   Scroll all the way to the bottom until you see:
   ```html
   <div class="big-number">1
   ```
   This is on **line 49**, right under a note.

4. **Change the number**
   Change it to any other number — up or down, it doesn't matter.

   *Example:*
   ```html
   <div class="big-number">1
   ```
   👆 In this example, `1` is the number you'd change.

5. **Commit your changes**
   Scroll back to the top and click **`Commit changes...`**

6. **Confirm**
   Wait a second for it to load, then click the next **Commit changes** button that appears.

7. **Done!** 🎉
   You'll be redirected — and that's it, your streak is safe.
